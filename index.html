About




Hi! I'm a first-year Masters student in the Jacobs Technion-Cornell Dual MSc Degree program focused in Urban Tech at Cornell Tech in NYC. My degree is focused on applying machine learning, data science, human-computer interaction, and product design to the social, economic and technical challenges of a city.

I graduated with a degree in International Economics from the University of British Columbia in May 2021. You can read my thesis (on environmental econ/int'l trade) here.

Throughout my undergrad, I developed a strong belief that making cities smarter while simultaneously being aware of the economic outcomes is a key part of a future-proof solution for climate change.
